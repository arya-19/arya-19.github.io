Nubra Valley: A Camping Hotspot For Adventure Seekers

Reading Time: 6 minutes
Written By Diya on 06/04

Known for its breathtaking landscapes, lovely walking trails, and the Shyok River, Nubra Valley is no less than paradise on Earth. Living in remoteness for most of the year, the valley comes to life during the summers. The grandiose Y-shaped Nubra Valley is, in fact, among the best places to visit in Leh Ladakh. Surrounded by rocky boulders and snow-clad mountains, the valley is a hotspot for trekkers and adventure travellers.

Take a trip to one of the most isolated valleys of North Ladakh to imbibe the beauty and serenity of this heavenly place in its purest form. Nubra Valley lies at a height of about 10,000 ft above sea level and is located at a distance of 150 km from Leh. The journey to the valley is an experience in itself as it allows you to experience the best of both nature and the simplistic lifestyles of the people residing in this beautiful place.

Switzerland’s Jungfraujoch: Going To The Top Of Europe

Reading Time: 4 minutes
Written By Smita on 06/04

Switzerland is one of the most beautiful places on earth that is home to several shimmering lakes, medieval towns, high peaks, and the highest mountain range in all of Europe (The Alps). All of this together with a plethora of breathtaking places makes Switzerland a top-notch travel destination for every traveller. But this time when you buy a Switzerland tour package, don’t restrict your trip to just sightseeing; make sure to spare a day or two for the thrilling and impressive journey to Jungfraujoch. Nicknamed as “Top of Europe”, this place is a heaven for adventure junkies and nature lovers. Perching at a height of 3,454 metres, Jungfraujoch is one of the highest tourist destinations of the continent.

The highlights of this place include the Aletsch Glacier, Jungfraujoch Railway Station, hiking hut-to-hut and the popular Ice Palace. Jungfraujoch is one of those spots of Switzerland that is overlooked by most travellers. This Bernese Alps’ summit unites the magnificent backdrop of ice and rocks with the awe-inspiring sights spread all over. The weather of Jungfraujoch, not surprisingly, is on the colder side throughout the year. So if you are planning on visiting here during your Swiss trip then do pack a good number of warmers.
